ABOUT CAROUSEL VIDEO

Invisible Spectrums

About

I conducted a short video around campus that revolved around the color palettes
on campus. The purpose was to display the “color” of student life through
architectural elements, scattered objects, hanging posters, natural life, etc., all of
which was ordered in a way to make it appear grey-toned and muted in the
beginning, and then more saturated near the end with brighter colors like blue,
green, yellow, and red making notable features.

For the short film, I focused on taking
many shots of still and moving life on
on campus. This way, I could arrange
them based on color palettes and
produce a kind of cohesion and
consistency that would be evident in
in the film to portray my story. You
find traces of life in every shot,
whether it be architectural art pieces,
the flow of fabric in the wind, or a
bouquet of assorted flowers, there is
character in every element that is
showcased.
